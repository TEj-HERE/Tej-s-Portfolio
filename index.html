About Me

Engineering with curiosity and craft.

I'm a B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering student at York University's Lassonde School of Engineering, having transferred from the University of Calgary with strong academic standing. I've been independently designing, building, and shipping robotics projects since age 13 -- 80+ builds, 124+ documented YouTube videos, 360,000+ views, and corporate sponsorships from JLCPCB and REYAX Technology. I design custom PCBs, build avionics systems for university rocketry team, and run JETech Labs -- a STEAM education startup I founded to bring hands-on robotics and electronics education to youth learners worldwide. Most recently, I took 1st place at the UNHACK 2025 Hackathon at York University competing against ~1,000 participants.
